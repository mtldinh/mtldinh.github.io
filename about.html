Hello! My name is Minh-Thu Dinh (she/her) and I am a second-year student studying electrical engineering with a concentration in Renewable Electric Energy Systems. I intend to become a field engineer that works directly with renewable energy and its respective power systems. I hope eventually, I can utilize my knowledge in engineering for political action towards a renewable and sustainable future.

To me, engineering is marrying the technological capabilities and human interaction. I believe working in energy is essential to curb future human damage to the environment and to future generations. I've performed research at Duke University with Dr. Steve Cummer on electromagnetic research through the NCSSM Summer Internship Program, developed an independent solar panel project through NCSSM's Research in Engineering Program, and worked on SolarPack for a competitive solar panel car. I am an upcoming Summer 2021 power systems intern for the power management company Eaton.
